Activities & Adventures

Experience the thrill of Nepal's diverse landscapes

Everest Region Activities

Journey to the base of the world's highest peak

Everest Base Camp Trek

The iconic trek to 5,364m, following in the footsteps of legendary mountaineers. Experience the Khumbu Icefall and Himalayan panorama.

Everest View Trek

A shorter alternative to EBC, offering stunning views of Everest, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam from the famous Everest View Hotel.

Namche Bazaar Exploration

Discover Sherpa culture in the vibrant Himalayan hub. Visit museums, monasteries, and enjoy local cuisine.

Sunrise at Everest View Hotel

Wake early to witness the golden light illuminate Everest and the surrounding peaks from Nepal's highest hotel.

Annapurna Region Activities

From lush forests to high mountain sanctuaries

Annapurna Base Camp Trek

Trek through diverse ecosystems to reach the sanctuary surrounded by Annapurna I, Machapuchare, and Hiunchuli.

Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek

Famous for its stunning sunrise views over the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges, through rhododendron forests.

Rhododendron Forest Walks

Best in spring when the hillsides explode with red, pink, and white blooms. Various trails available.

Gurung Village Visits

Experience traditional Gurung culture in villages like Ghandruk and Chhomrong, known for their hospitality.

Pokhara Adventure Hub

Nepal's adventure capital

Paragliding

Soar above Phewa Lake and the Annapurna range with tandem flights. A bucket-list experience for thrill-seekers.

Ultra-Light Flights

Fly above Pokhara Valley for breathtaking aerial views of mountains, lakes, and waterfalls.

ZipLine & Bungee

Experience Nepal's highest zip line and bungee jump at various locations around Pokhara.

Sarangkot Sunrise

Wake early for breathtaking sunrise views over the Annapurna range reflected in Phewa Lake.

World Peace Pagoda Visit

Explore the stunning Buddhist stupa above Pokhara with panoramic mountain views.

Boating on Phewa Lake

Relax on the calm waters of Phewa Lake, visit Tal Barahi Temple on an island in the lake.

Manang Valley Activities

High-altitude adventure and culture

Annapurna Circuit Sections

Part of the classic circuit trek through the Manang Valley with stunning mountain passes.

Ice Lake Hike

A rewarding day hike to the sacred glacial lake at 4,600m with panoramic mountain views.

Glacier Walks

Explore the ancient glaciers around Manang with experienced local guides.

Tibetan Monastery Visits

Visit the centuries-old Braga Monastery and other spiritual sites in the valley.

Mustang Activities

Explore the ancient forbidden kingdom

Kagbeni Exploration

Wander through the medieval streets of Kagbeni, a gateway to Upper Mustang.

Jomsom Village Walk

Explore the windy town of Jomsom and its unique apple orchards and culture.

Upper Mustang Trek

For the adventurous, the restricted area of Upper Mustang offers unique Tibetan culture.

Cave Explorations

Discover ancient caves carved into the cliffs, including the mysterious sky caves.

Rara Lake Activities

Serenity in remote western Nepal

Hiking Trails

Various trails around the lake offering different perspectives of this pristine destination.

Jungle Walks

Explore the surrounding forests rich with wildlife including musk deer and Himalayan black bears.

Bird Watching

Spot rare species including the spiny babler and Himalayan monal in the surrounding wilderness.

Camping & Photography

Set up camp by the lake for unforgettable sunrises and star-filled nights.

Shey Phoksundo Activities

Nepal's deepest lake and ancient culture

Trek to Phoksundo Lake

The highlight of Dolpo region, the turquoise waters of Phoksundo are truly mesmerizing.

Ringmo Village Visit

Explore the traditional Dolpo-pa village with its stone houses and Buddhist traditions.

Bon Monastery Visit

Visit one of the few Bon monasteries in Nepal, learn about the pre-Buddhist religion.

Cliffside Viewpoints

Hike to viewpoints overlooking the lake for spectacular photos of the ringed lake.

Lumbini Activities

Spiritual pilgrimage and peace

Meditation Retreats

Join meditation programs at various monasteries in the peaceful monastic zones.

Maya Devi Temple

Visit the exact birthplace of Buddha, with the Marker Stone and Ashokan Pillar.

International Monasteries

Explore over 25 monasteries built by different countries, each with unique architecture.

Cycling Tours

Rent a bicycle to explore the vast Lumbini Garden and surrounding villages.

Ama Yangri Activities

Quick escape from Kathmandu

Sunrise Hike

The main attraction - hike to the peak for golden sunrise over the Himalayas.

Langtang Himalayan Views

On clear days, enjoy views of Langtang, Ganesh Himal, and the entire central Himalayas.

Sherpa Village Experience

Visit traditional Helambu Sherpa villages and experience their warm hospitality.

Forest Trails

Walk through rhododendron and oak forests on well-maintained trails.
