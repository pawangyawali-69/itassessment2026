Travel Itineraries

Plan your perfect Nepal adventure with our detailed guides

Everest Region

5-10 days journey to the roof of the world

Classic EBC Trek (8-10 days)

Day 1Fly Kathmandu → Lukla (2,840m)
Day 2Lukla → Phakding (2,610m) - 3 hours
Day 3Phakding → Namche Bazaar (3,440m) - 5 hours
Day 4Acclimatization day in Namche
Day 5Namche → Tengboche (3,860m) - 5 hours
Day 6Tengboche → Dingboche (4,410m) - 5 hours
Day 7Acclimatization in Dingboche
Day 8Dingboche → Lobuche (4,940m) - 4 hours
Day 9Lobuche → EBC (5,364m) → Gorakshep
Day 10Gorakshep → Kalapatthar → Pheriche → Namche

Everest View Trek (5-6 days)

A shorter alternative focusing on Sherpa culture and Himalayan views without the altitude challenges of EBC.

Day 1Fly Kathmandu → Lukla → Phakding
Day 2Phakding → Namche Bazaar
Day 3Namche → Everest View Hotel (3,880m)
Day 4Sunrise at Everest View Hotel
Day 5-6Return via Lukla

Annapurna Region

Diverse landscapes from rhododendron forests to high altitudes

Ghorepani Poon Hill (4-5 days)

Day 1Drive Pokhara → Tikhedhunga
Day 2Tikhedhunga → Ghorepani (2,850m)
Day 3Poon Hill sunrise → Tadapani
Day 4Tadapani → Ghandruk → Nayapul

Annapurna Base Camp (7-10 days)

Day 1Pokhara → Nayapul → Tikhedhunga
Day 2Tikhedhunga → Ghorepani
Day 3Ghorepani → Poon Hill sunrise → ABC (4,130m)
Day 4-5Explore ABC and Machapuchare base
Day 6-7Return via Chhomrong
