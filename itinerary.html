Travel Itineraries

Plan your perfect Nepal adventure with our detailed guides

Mustang

The forbidden kingdom awaits

Mustang Explorer (5-7 days)

Day 1Fly Pokhara → Jomsom (2,720m)
Day 2Jomsom → Kagbeni (2,980m)
Day 3Kagbeni → Muktinath (3,800m)
Day 4Explore Jharkot & Muktinath
Day 5-7Return to Pokhara

Rara Lake

Nepal's largest and most beautiful lake

Rara Lake Trek (4-6 days)

Day 1Fly Kathmandu → Nepalgunj
Day 2Drive Nepalgunj → Jumla (2,540m)
Day 3Jumla → Rara Lake (3,002m)
Day 4Explore Rara Lake & hiking
Day 5-6Return to Kathmandu

Shey Phoksundo

Deepest lake in Nepal with unique culture

Shey Phoksundo Trek (6-9 days)

Day 1Fly Kathmandu → Nepalgunj
Day 2Drive Nepalgunj → Juphal (2,440m)
Day 3Juphal → Dunai → Suligad
Day 4-5Trek to Phoksundo Lake (3,611m)
Day 6Ringmo village & Bon monastery
Day 7-9Return via same route

Lumbini

The birthplace of Buddha

Lumbini Pilgrimage (1-2 days)

Day 1Arrive Lumbini, visit Maya Devi Temple
Day 2Monastic zones, Ashokan Pillar, meditation

Ama Yangri

Highest vehicle-accessible peak near Kathmandu

Ama Yangri Weekend (2 days)

Day 1Drive Kathmandu → Sundarijal → Tarkeghyang
Day 2Sunrise hike to Ama Yangri peak (3,189m)
