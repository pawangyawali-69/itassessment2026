About Us

Our story and commitment to exceptional travel experiences

Our Story

Discover Nepal was founded with a singular vision: to share the incredible beauty and rich culture of Nepal with the world. As a locally-based travel company, we have deep roots in the communities we serve and extensive knowledge of Nepal's diverse landscapes.

From the bustling streets of Kathmandu to the serene heights of the Himalayas, we believe that every traveler deserves an authentic, memorable experience. Our team of expert guides and travel specialists are passionate about showcasing the best of Nepal while respecting local traditions and environmental preservation.

Over the years, we have helped thousands of travelers discover the magic of Nepal - from first-time visitors to seasoned trekkers seeking new adventures. Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us one of Nepal's most trusted travel partners.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional travel experiences that showcase Nepal's natural beauty, rich heritage, and vibrant culture while promoting sustainable tourism and supporting local communities.

Our Vision

To be Nepal's most trusted travel partner, creating lifelong memories for every traveler and contributing to the preservation of Nepal's unique environment and culture for future generations.

Why Nepal Tourism

Nepal offers unparalleled experiences that draw travelers from around the world

Himalayan Majesty

Home to 8 of the world's 10 highest peaks including Mount Everest. The Himalayas offer trekking experiences found nowhere else on Earth.

Rich Heritage

Ancient temples, sacred monasteries, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Nepal is a living museum of Himalayan culture and spirituality.

Diverse Landscapes

From tropical forests to alpine tundra, Nepal packs incredible geographical diversity into its compact territory.

Adventure Activities

Trekking, mountaineering, rafting, paragliding, and more. Nepal is the adventure capital of the world.
